Electronic Life (Aug 2022)

On the 6th of August 2022, an 11 year old me was on an iPad in the apartment of my nan & granddad, it was evening from what I remember, I went on GarageBand and started to play around with the electronic drums on there, I didn't even know what bpm was back then. The reason the songs are so off-rhythm is because I screenrecorded the beats and cut the bars in the screenrecordings to where I thought they would approximately be.
